Admissions

Galgotias University(GU), Uttar Pradesh degree, diploma and integrated programmes at the undergraduate(UG), postgraduate(PG) and doctorate level(PhD). The programmes offered  are in the streams of - Engineering, Science, IT & Software, Architecture & Planning, Business & Management Studies, Accounting & Commerce, Humanities & Social Sciences, Medicine & Health Sciences, Nursing, Mass Communication & Media, Hospitality & Travel, Law, Teaching & Education

Besides these, the university also offers UG and PG programmes in Library Science, Applied Science, Clinical Research, Logistics & Aviation Management.

Minimum eligibility requirements for UG and PG courses

For admissions to UG programmes offered by the university, students must have passed 10+2 or equivalent with a minimum of 45% to 60% aggregate marks depending on the course. For some programmes, subject combination and age of the candidate will also be considered.

Students will be considered eligible for admissions to PG programmes at GU if they have completed their graduation with a minimum of 45% to 60% aggregate marks depending on the course. For some programmes, subject combination and age of the candidate will also be taken into consideration.

For integrated programmes, students should have passed Class XII with a minimum of 45% to 60% marks in four relevant subjects.

Students will be able to pursue diploma programmes at GU if they have passed Class 10 with a minimum 35% to 40% of marks depending on the course.

Note: Eligibility criteria will vary from course to course. Thus, aspirants are advised to go thoroughly through the eligibility criteria of the programme they are seeking admission to.

Scholarships

Tuition fee waivers offered by Galgotias University are as follows:
1. Toppers (1st Rank Holder) of all the Board/ University - 100% tuition fee waiver
2. Students securing up to 4000 rank in the IIT/ JEE Examination or up to 100 rank in the UPSEE Examination - 50% tuition fee waiver
3. Students securing 93% aggregate/ PCM and above in class 12 or 80% marks in graduation - 25% tuition fee waiver
4. Students securing up to 5000 rank in JEE-Mains Examination or scoring at least 70% tile in CAT or above 700 marks in MAT - 20% tuition fee waiver
5. Students having secured gold medal or silver medal at the national level/state level in sports or have some exemplary achievements to their credit in the area of performing arts - 50% tuition fee waiver
